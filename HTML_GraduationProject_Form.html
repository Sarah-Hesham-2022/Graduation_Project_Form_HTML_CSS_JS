Plants' Disease Detection and Classification

                                                                                                               

Can computer vision help scientists and researchers identify a plant type and detect the type and position of its diseases from the leaves, if it wasn't healthy? The answer is, “yes”. Plants' diseases detection and classification have always been problems of big concern and now can be solved using artificial intelligence for smart agriculture applications, this "yes" lies beneath the umbrella of artificial intelligence science, that is the center of this era and the main scope of the future as well.

We are Computer Science and Bioinformatics Undergarduate Seniors at Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, Cairo University.

Our graduation project for year 2023 is about "Smart Agriculture" .

Automatic detection of plant diseases shows promising benefits in monitoring large fields of crops by detecting the diseases as soon as they appear on plant leaves.

The aim of the system is to introduce a software solution for automatic detection and classification of plant leaves' diseases using their images.
